Noir Investment Group

The Noir Investment Group is an idea borne out of watching how the business world is evolving. While the reasons for decentralization and the uses of crypto currencies have been discussed at length, real world integration and business application of these ideas has not been truly explored. The NIG seeks to progress and develop in this direction in the hopes of expanding and gaining sizeable interest and profit through entrepreneurship in this new branch of e-commerce and crypto industry. By combing the nature of decentralized crypto currencies and corporate structures to create a diverse machine, we have defined a new type of business whose application and reach is almost boundless. The ideas surrounding this venture are in many ways similar to existing ones but with innovative and implementable ideas not based on suppositions and hedged bets. By implementing visible structures, enforceable rules and useable policies we seek to maximize productivity and maintain a level of openness that allows fluid work process and easy auditing by investors. The venture will be defined at first by milestones which as we become larger and more experienced will turn into time-framed tasks. With each of these milestones being met will be the realization of steps towards developing an industry leading enterprise that reaps great profits from its ventures in both the goods and the services sectors. While we will not draw absolute lines on which kind of ventures the group will not pursue, it must be made clear that we will not support any form of pointless enterprise whose value is “ just a flash in the pan “ so to speak. All candidates will be vetted rigorously and o effort or resources will be wasted before a Cost/Benefit and Risk Assessment has been carried out. From there it falls to the group to decide on execution or rejection. Some of the restrictions that will be on the enterprise are that in the beginning there is no real investor confidence, short of those wish to support the idea or who will take speculative/calculated risk. As a result some of the larger projects will not be initiated until we have stabilized investor growth and our structures have been tried and tested.
